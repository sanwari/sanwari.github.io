Configuring mGBA

mGBA Keyboard Configuration

While mGBA is running, click on "mGBA" in the menubar and then click on "preferences".

In the side menu, click on "Keyboard". in here you can map your keyboard to the controls for mGBA.

mGBA Controller Configuration

mGBA also gives you the option to connect a controller! You can map the buttons from your controller to the controls for mGBA.

mGBA supports both Bluetooth Controllers as well as plug and play controllers!

The configuration page for controllers is under "Controller" in "Preferences".

Configuring VisualBoyAdvanced

VisualBoyAdvanced Keyboard Configuration

While VisualBoyAdvanced is running, click on "options in the menubar. Then, hover over "joypad. After that, hover over "Configure" and click on "1".

A form should pop up titled "Joypad configuration". In here, you can map your keyboard to the controls for VisualBoyAdvanced.

One cool thing VisualBoyAdvanced has is the fact that you can configure multiple different key mappings! VisualBoyAdvanced gives you the option to configure up to 4 diffent key mappings!
